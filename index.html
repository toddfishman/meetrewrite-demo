Rewrite turns your site, web app, or storefront into a real Swift / SwiftUI app — not a WebView wrapper. This is an interactive demo of the actual 13-step guided run: scan & scope → preview & quote → build & handoff. Preview free. Pay when it ships.

~5 min · no signup · 3 sample projects

How it works

Three phases. Thirteen steps. One Xcode project at the end.
PHASE 1 · STEPS 1–6
Scan & scope
Drop a live URL (and optionally your code). Rewrite captures how it looks today, scans routes, components and design tokens, then you set scope & direction.
PHASE 2 · STEPS 7–9
Preview & quote
See free native previews of your screens on a real iPhone frame. Choose a delivery tier — scaffold or fully-wired — and approve a fixed quote before anything is built.
PHASE 3 · STEPS 10–13
Build & handoff
Native screens are built and reviewed, balance settles, and you get a buildable Xcode project plus a plain-English path to TestFlight and the App Store.
